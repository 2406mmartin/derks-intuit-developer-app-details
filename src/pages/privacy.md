# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 22, 2026

Application Name: Derks Financial QBO Internal Tool  
Company Name: Derks Financial  
Contact: ryan@derksfinancial.com

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Derks Financial collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with Derks Financial QBO Internal Tool.

Derks Financial QBO Internal Tool is a private internal business tool used by Derks Financial to connect with and manage the company’s own QuickBooks Online account and related business workflows.

The application is not intended for public use by unrelated third parties.

## 2. Information We Access

The application may access information from QuickBooks Online and related business systems when authorized by Derks Financial.

This information may include business and accounting data such as customers, vendors, accounts, transactions, invoices, payments, deposits, bills, purchase records, reports, company information, and related accounting records.

The application may also collect technical information needed to operate the service, such as user account information, access logs, authentication tokens, error logs, device or browser information, and usage activity within the application.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information accessed through the application only for internal business purposes.

These purposes may include accounting, reconciliation, transaction review, reporting, workflow automation, administrative support, financial record organization, customer or vendor record review, internal analytics, troubleshooting, security monitoring, and improving the application.

We do not sell QuickBooks Online data or business accounting data.

## 4. QuickBooks Online Data

When the application connects to QuickBooks Online, it uses authorized access through Intuit’s developer platform and APIs.

The application only accesses QuickBooks Online data that has been authorized by Derks Financial.

QuickBooks Online data is used only to support the internal business operations of Derks Financial.

## 5. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade information accessed through the application.

We may share information only when necessary for internal business operations, compliance, security, or operation of the application.

Information may be shared with authorized employees, contractors, representatives, or service providers who need access to perform work for Derks Financial.

Service providers may include hosting providers, cloud infrastructure providers, database providers, authentication providers, software tools, or technical support providers.

We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, security, or property of Derks Financial.

## 6. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information accessed through the application.

These safeguards may include access controls, authentication, encryption where appropriate, limited user permissions, secure storage, monitoring, and review of access.

No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss.

## 7. Access Tokens and Credentials

The application may store authentication tokens or credentials needed to maintain an authorized connection with QuickBooks Online or related services.

These tokens are used only to operate the application and maintain authorized access.

Access to tokens and credentials is limited to authorized systems and personnel.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for internal business purposes, legal obligations, accounting requirements, security, troubleshooting, or operation of the application.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and business need.

When information is no longer needed, we may delete, archive, anonymize, or securely retain it according to our internal practices and legal obligations.

## 9. User Access and Controls

Authorized users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of certain information by contacting Derks Financial.

Some information may need to be retained for legal, accounting, security, or business reasons.

Access to the application may be modified, restricted, or revoked at any time by Derks Financial.

## 10. Disconnecting QuickBooks Online

Derks Financial may disconnect the application from QuickBooks Online at any time through the application, through QuickBooks Online settings, or by contacting the application administrator.

After disconnection, the application will no longer be able to access new QuickBooks Online data unless access is authorized again.

Some previously accessed information may remain stored if needed for internal business, legal, accounting, security, or operational purposes.

## 11. Children’s Privacy

The application is intended for business use only.

It is not directed to children and is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13.

## 12. International Use

The application is operated for internal business purposes by Derks Financial.

If information is accessed, processed, or stored outside of the user’s location, it will be handled according to this Privacy Policy and applicable law.

## 13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When we update it, we will revise the “Last Updated” date above.

Continued use of the application after changes means the updated Privacy Policy applies.
